Royal Lahaina Luau

The Royal Lahaina Luau, also known as the Myths of Maui Luau, on the island of Maui, Hawaii, is conveniently located in Ka'anapali, just 10 minutes from Lahaina at the Royal Lahaina Resort. It is Maui's longest running Polynesian luau. Guests enjoy a delicious luau buffet dinner with an open bar, cultural presentations and a Polynesian revue featuring Hawaiian, Samoan, Tahitian and Maori music and dances.
