Royal Lahaina Luau Directions and Location

  • Location

    Oceanfront Luau Gardens at Royal Lahaina Resort, 2780 Keka'a Drive, Ka'anapali, West Maui. Directions can be found here

  • Is seating assigned?

    Seating is assigned. However, you will be able to upgrade to first row seating for an additional amount. Preferred Seating also gets you first in line for the buffet. Not all Maui luaus have preferred seating, but the Royal Lahaina Luau in Ka'anapali does!

  • Wheelchair Accessibility?

    Facilities at the Royal Lahaina Resort and Luau are wheelchair accessible.
